<p><p>Tankless electric water heaters, also known as on-demand water heaters, are water heating systems that provide hot water on demand, without the need for a storage tank. These units heat water by passing it through electric coils, resulting in continuous hot water supply whenever needed.</p><p>The market for tankless electric water heaters is currently witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue this trend in the future. The increasing need for energy-efficient and cost-effective water heating systems, coupled with advancements in technology, is driving the demand for tankless electric water heaters.</p><p>One of the key drivers for the market is the rising awareness regarding the environmental impact of traditional water heaters, which consume a significant amount of energy. Tankless electric water heaters are highly efficient, as they only heat water when needed, eliminating standby heat loss. This energy efficiency, combined with their compact size and longer lifespan, makes them an attractive choice for residential and commercial applications.</p><p>The growing trend of smart homes and integration of IoT in various household appliances is also expected to boost the market. Tankless electric water heaters can be easily integrated into smart home systems, enabling users to control and monitor settings remotely, thus adding to their convenience and energy-saving capabilities.</p><p>Furthermore, government initiatives and regulations promoting energy efficiency and the adoption of sustainable technologies are expected to drive the market growth.
