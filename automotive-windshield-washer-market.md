<p><p>The automotive windshield washer market consists of two main types: electrical and mechanical. Electrical windshield washers are powered by an electric motor, which pumps the washer fluid onto the windshield through a series of nozzles. This type of washer is commonly found in modern vehicles as it offers more convenience and control. On the other hand, mechanical windshield washers are powered by the vehicle's manual operation, usually using a hand-operated pump. These types of washers are generally seen in older or simpler vehicles where electrical systems may not be available or preferred.</p></p>
