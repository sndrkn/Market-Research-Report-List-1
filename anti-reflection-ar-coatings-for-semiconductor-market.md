<p><p>Anti-Reflection (AR) coatings for the semiconductor market are used to minimize reflection and enhance light transmission in various applications. Bottom Anti-Reflective Coating (BARC) is applied underneath the photoresist layer to reduce reflection during lithography processes. Top Anti-Reflective Coating (TARC) is used above the photoresist layer to ensure accurate pattern transfer. Silicon Anti-Reflective Coating (Si-ARC) is used on silicon substrates to reduce reflection and improve optical performance. Carbon Coating is applied to resistive layers for etching, preventing light reflection and enabling precise etching processes in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
