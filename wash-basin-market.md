<p><p>A wash basin, also known as a sink or hand basin, is a plumbing fixture used for washing hands and other small personal hygiene tasks. The market is primarily driven by the increasing demand for modern and stylish bathroom fixtures, coupled with rising consumer disposable income. The commercial sector, including hotels, restaurants, and offices, is a major consumer of wash basins due to the high footfall and demand for premium facilities.</p><p>Latest Trends:</p><p>The wash basin market is witnessing various trends that are shaping its growth. Firstly, there is a growing preference for eco-friendly and water-saving wash basins as a result of increasing environmental concerns. Manufacturers are incorporating innovative designs and technologies to minimize water wastage without compromising the functionality.</p><p>Secondly, there is a rising demand for touchless or sensor-operated wash basins in public spaces, healthcare facilities, and high-end residential projects to promote hygiene and prevent the spread of infections.
