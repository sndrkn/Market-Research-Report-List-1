<p><p>The nanogel vacuum insulated panel market is categorized into three main types: silica, fiberglass, and others. Silica nanogel panels are composed of silica aerogel, which offers excellent thermal insulation properties due to its low thermal conductivity. Fiberglass nanogel panels consist of fiberglass materials embedded with nanogel particles, providing both thermal insulation and structural strength. The "others" category includes various nanogel panels made from different materials such as carbon, polymer, and metal composites. These nanogel vacuum insulated panels are extensively used in construction, transportation, cold chain logistics, and other industries to enhance energy efficiency and reduce heat transfer.</p></p>
