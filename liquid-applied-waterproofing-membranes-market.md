<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The liquid applied waterproofing membranes market consists of three main types: bituminous, elastomeric, and cementitious membranes. Bituminous membranes are manufactured using bitumen, a black sticky substance, and provide excellent waterproofing properties. Elastomeric membranes are made from synthetic polymers, offering flexibility and durability. They are resistant to UV radiation and can stretch to accommodate structural movements. Cementitious membranes are composed of cement and other additives, providing high flexibility and resistance to water penetration. All three types of membranes are commonly used in construction to provide effective waterproofing solutions.</p></p>
