<p><p>The automotive battery protection IC market consists of two major types: secondary protection and overcharge protection. Secondary protection is aimed at preventing potential damages caused by abnormal conditions such as short circuits, overcurrent, and overheating of batteries. It ensures the overall safety and performance of the battery pack. On the other hand, overcharge protection prevents batteries from overcharging, which can lead to reduced battery life or even explosions. Both types of protection play a vital role in safeguarding automotive battery systems and improving their lifespan and reliability.</p></p>
