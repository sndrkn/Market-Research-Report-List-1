<p><p>The gravure ink market consists of four main types: water type, alcoholic type, benzene type, and petrol type. Water type gravure ink is water-based and environmentally friendly, commonly used for printing on food packaging. Alcoholic type gravure ink contains alcohol as a solvent and is suitable for printing on various plastic films. Benzene type gravure ink uses benzene as a solvent and is used for printing on metalized films. Petrol type gravure ink uses petroleum-based solvents and is ideal for high-speed printing on plastic films. Each type caters to specific printing requirements and substrates.</p></p>
