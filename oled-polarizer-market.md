<p><p>The OLED polarizer market is categorized into different types, including circular polarizers, square polarizers, and others. Circular polarizers are designed to rotate the polarization of light in all directions, allowing for optimum visibility regardless of the screen's orientation. Square polarizers, on the other hand, are used in displays that require a specific polarization orientation, such as LCD panels. The "others" category encompasses any polarizer variants that do not fall into the circular or square types, fulfilling diverse industry requirements. These different market types offer flexibility and options for various OLED display applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The OLED polarizer market finds application in various types of OLED displays.
