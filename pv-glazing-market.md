This allows for real-time monitoring of energy generation and consumption, resulting in optimized building performance.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the use of building-integrated PV glazing. Transparent Conductive Oxide (TCO) Coated PV Glazing has a thin conductive layer that aids in current flow. Annealed PV Glazing is regular glass without any additional treatments.
