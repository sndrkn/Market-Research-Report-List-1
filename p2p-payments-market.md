<p><p>P2P payments, also known as peer-to-peer payments, refer to the transfer of funds from one individual to another through a digital platform, eliminating the need for traditional intermediaries such as banks. This market has gained significant traction in recent years due to factors such as the rapid growth of mobile and internet usage, increasing adoption of digital wallets, and the convenience and simplicity it offers to users. Additionally, the ongoing digitization of financial services, along with the increasing trend of cashless transactions, further contributes to the market's growth.</p><p>The COVID-19 pandemic has also accelerated the adoption of P2P payments as people seek contactless payment options and avoid physical interactions. This shift in consumer behavior is expected to further drive the market's growth in the coming years.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in technology, such as the integration of P2P payments with social messaging platforms and the use of blockchain technology for secure transactions, are expected to fuel market growth.
