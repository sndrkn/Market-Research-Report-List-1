<p><p>The LLDPE stretch film market consists of two main types: hand film and machine film. Hand film is used for manual packaging and is typically wrapped around items using hand-held dispensers. It is commonly used for smaller loads or irregularly shaped products. On the other hand, machine film is utilized with automated wrapping machines and is suitable for larger loads or high-volume packaging operations. Both types of stretch films provide secure and cost-effective packaging solutions for various industries.</p></p>
