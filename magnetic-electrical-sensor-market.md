<p><strong>The Magnetic Electrical Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Effect Type</li><li>AMR Type</li><li>GMR Type</li><li>Other Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magnetic Electrical Sensor market consists of various types such as Hall Effect Type, AMR Type, GMR Type, and Other Type. The Hall Effect Type utilizes a semiconductor material and measures the voltage generated when exposed to a magnetic field. The AMR Type employs the anisotropic magnetoresistance effect to detect changes in resistance caused by a magnetic field. The GMR Type relies on the giant magnetoresistance effect to detect variations in electrical resistance due to the presence of a magnetic field. The Other Type includes various magnetic sensors with different working principles.</p></p>
