<p><p>The hypersport motorcycle tires market consists of two main types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires are designed with steel belts that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing excellent stability at high speeds and precise handling. They offer better grip and enhanced cornering abilities, making them popular among sport bike riders. On the other hand, bias tires have plies that run diagonally across the tire, offering a more flexible sidewall and providing a comfortable ride. Bias tires are commonly used in touring motorcycles due to their durability and resistance to punctures.</p></p>
