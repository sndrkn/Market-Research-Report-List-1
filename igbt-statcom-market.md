These STATCOMs are utilized for voltage control, power quality improvement, and reactive power compensation in various industries and utility applications.</p><p>High Voltage STATCOM, on the other hand, operates in high voltage power systems, usually above 132kV. They are employed for grid stabilization, reducing transmission losses, and ensuring grid reliability by managing fluctuating reactive power demands. These devices play a crucial role in addressing voltage stability issues in the electrical grids.</p></p>
