<p><p>Sheet mica is classified into two market types: natural mica and synthetic mica. Natural mica is obtained from mines and is widely used due to its high heat resistance, excellent electrical insulation properties, and optical clarity. It is commonly used in industries such as electronics, automotive, and construction. Synthetic mica, on the other hand, is artificially produced using chemical processes and has properties similar to natural mica. It is preferred in some applications due to its consistent quality, cost-effectiveness, and reduced environmental impact. Both natural and synthetic mica find diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>
