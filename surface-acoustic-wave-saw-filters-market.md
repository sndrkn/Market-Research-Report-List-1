<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Filters are used in various industries for signal processing and frequency control applications. There are two main types of SAW filters: Transversal Filters and Resonator Filters. Transversal Filters are used for frequency selection and are capable of separating signals with different frequencies. On the other hand, Resonator Filters have resonating structures that allow them to efficiently select and filter out specific frequencies. Both types of SAW filters play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
