<p><p>Polyester Films are widely used in various industries for their versatility, durability, and cost-effectiveness. There are two main types: Rigid Polyester Films and Flexible Polyester Films. Rigid Polyester Films are stiff and commonly used for packaging, graphic arts, and electrical insulation applications. They provide excellent dimensional stability and high tensile strength. On the other hand, Flexible Polyester Films are more pliable and suitable for applications like lamination, labeling, and printing. They offer good heat resistance and flexibility, making them ideal for flexible packaging solutions. Both types contribute significantly to the growth of the Polyester Films market.</p></p>
