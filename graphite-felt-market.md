<p><p>Graphite felt is a versatile material used in various industries. It is available in two types: soft felt and rigid felt. Soft graphite felt is flexible and easily moldable, making it suitable for applications that require conformability, such as insulation. Rigid graphite felt, on the other hand, is stiffer and provides more structural integrity, making it ideal for applications that require strength and stability, such as electrodes in fuel cells. Both types have different characteristics and are used in different sectors based on their specific properties and requirements.</p></p>
