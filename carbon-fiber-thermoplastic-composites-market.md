They are used in various industries due to their high strength, stiffness, and lightweight properties. Short Fiber Thermoplastic Composites consist of carbon fibers that are randomly distributed within the thermoplastic matrix. Long Fiber Thermoplastic Composites have longer carbon fibers that provide better mechanical performance. Continuous Fiber Thermoplastic Composites have continuous carbon fibers, resulting in even higher strength and stiffness. Glass Mat Thermoplastic Composites incorporate a layer of glass fibers for enhanced impact resistance and durability.</p></p>
