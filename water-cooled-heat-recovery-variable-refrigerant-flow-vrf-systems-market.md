<p><p>Water-cooled Heat Recovery Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) Systems are advanced HVAC systems that offer energy efficiency and simultaneous heating and cooling capabilities. There are two types of market systems: 2 Pipe Systems and 3 Pipe Systems. 2 Pipe Systems use a single piping network for both heating and cooling, which can cause some spaces to be too hot or too cold simultaneously. In contrast, 3 Pipe Systems use separate piping for heating and cooling, allowing for precise temperature control and eliminating the issue of simultaneous hot and cold spots.</p></p>
