<p><p>Synthetic cutting oil is a type of lubricant used in industrial machining processes to reduce friction and heat. There are two main types: water-based and oil-based cutting oils. Water-based cutting oils are made by mixing water with special additives, providing good cooling properties but limited lubrication. On the other hand, oil-based cutting oils are made from high-quality mineral or synthetic oils, offering excellent lubrication and extended tool life. Both types have their unique advantages and are chosen based on the specific machining requirements, such as materials being worked on and desired finish.</p></p>
