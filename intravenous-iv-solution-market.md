<p><strong>The Intravenous (IV) Solution Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Total Parenteral Solution</li><li>Peripheral Parenteral Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intravenous (IV) solution market is divided into two main types: Total Parenteral Solution (TPN) and Peripheral Parenteral Solution (PPN). TPN refers to a type of IV solution that provides all the necessary nutrients, electrolytes, and fluids required by patients who are unable to consume food orally. This solution is administered directly into a large central vein. On the other hand, PPN is a type of IV solution that provides a lower concentration of nutrients and is administered through a peripheral vein in patients who can partially intake food orally.</p></p>
