<p><p>The film for capacitor market consists of various types, including BOPP capacitor film, BOPET capacitor film, and others. Similarly, BOPET capacitor film is made from biaxially oriented polyester, offering excellent mechanical strength and electrical insulation properties. The "others" category includes additional types of films used in capacitors, such as paper, mica, and ceramic films. These films play a vital role in ensuring reliable and efficient performance of capacitors in various applications.</p></p>
