<p><p>Mobile vehicle barriers are security systems designed to prevent unauthorized access to a premises by vehicles. There are two types of mobile vehicle barriers: modular and non-modular. Modular mobile vehicle barriers are made up of separate units that can be connected to form a larger barrier. This flexibility allows for customization and reconfiguration as per the needs of the location. Non-modular mobile vehicle barriers, on the other hand, are fixed barriers that cannot be easily modified. Both types of barriers provide effective protection against vehicle-related threats and enhance security measures.</p></p>
