<p><p>Anaerobic thread sealants are a type of adhesive used to seal and lock threaded joints of pipes and fittings. They come in three types: low, medium, and high strength. Low strength sealants are ideal for small-sized pipes and fittings, requiring easy disconnection or adjustment. Medium strength sealants are suitable for a wide range of applications, providing a balance between ease of disassembly and resistance to leakage. High strength sealants are designed for heavy-duty applications, where a secure and permanent seal is necessary, preventing leakage even under extreme conditions.</p></p>
