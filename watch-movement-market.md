<p><p>The watch movement market consists of three main types: manual winding, automatic, and quartz movements. Manual winding movements require the wearer to manually wind the watch daily to keep it running. Automatic movements use the natural motion of the wearer's wrist to power the watch, eliminating the need for manual winding. Quartz movements rely on a battery that sends electrical signals through a quartz crystal, causing it to vibrate and power the watch. Each movement type caters to different preferences and lifestyles, offering diverse options for watch enthusiasts.</p></p>
