<p><p>Resorbable lifting thread market types include notched and smooth variations. Notched threads are composed of barbs or notches along their length, allowing for a stronger grip when inserted into the skin. These threads create a lifting effect by attaching to the deep layers of tissue. On the other hand, smooth threads lack barbs and rely on the principle of adhesion to lift and tighten the skin. Both types offer non-surgical options for facial rejuvenation and provide temporary lifting effects, gradually dissolving over time.</p></p>
