<p><p>The parking meter market consists of two main types: single space parking meters and multi-space parking kiosks. Single space parking meters are standalone devices that are installed next to individual parking spaces. They usually accept coins or credit cards for payment and display the amount of time remaining. On the other hand, multi-space parking kiosks serve multiple parking spaces in a particular area. They allow users to pay for parking at a central kiosk, usually using various payment options, and then display the ticket or receipt on their vehicle's dashboard.</p></p>
