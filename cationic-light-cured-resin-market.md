<p><p>The cationic light cured resin market consists of various types of components, including oligomers, monomers, photoinitiators, and additives. Oligomers are large molecules composed of several repeating units, while monomers are smaller molecules that can combine to form oligomers. Photoinitiators are chemicals that start the curing process when exposed to light. Additives are substances added to the resin formulation to enhance certain properties. These components work together to create a cationic light cured resin with specific properties and characteristics, which can be used in various applications such as adhesives, coatings, and dental materials.</p></p>
