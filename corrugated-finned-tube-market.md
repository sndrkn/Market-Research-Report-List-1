<p><p>The corrugated finned tube market consists of two main types: single metal finned tubes and bimetal composite finned tubes. Single metal finned tubes are made from a single material and are commonly used in heat exchangers and air coolers. On the other hand, bimetal composite finned tubes are made from two different metals, combining the benefits of both materials. These tubes are known for their high heat transfer efficiency and are commonly used in industries such as petrochemical, power, and HVAC.</p></p>
