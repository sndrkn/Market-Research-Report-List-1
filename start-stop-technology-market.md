<p><p>Start-Stop Technology is a feature in some vehicles that automatically shuts off the engine when the vehicle comes to a stop and then restarts it when the brake is released. There are different market types for this technology: </p><p>1. Enhanced starter: A more advanced version of the traditional starter that provides quicker and smoother engine restarts.</p><p>2. Belt-driven alternator starter (BAS): Uses a belt-driven system to combine the functions of an alternator and a starter motor.</p><p>3. Direct starter: A compact and lightweight starter that integrates seamlessly into the engine.</p><p>4. Integrated starter generator (ISG): Combines the functions of a starter and a generator into a single unit, providing more efficient engine start and power generation.</p></p>
