Hydroxycarboxylic Acids, such as citric acid, are effective chelants used in detergents and personal care products. Organophosphonates, like phosphoric acid derivatives, are commonly used in water treatment and industrial cleaning applications. Lastly, "Others" refers to less common types of chelants, including amino acids and lignins, which find application in specific niche markets.</p></p>
