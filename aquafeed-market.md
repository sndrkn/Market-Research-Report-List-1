<p><strong>The AquaFeed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mash Feed</li><li>Pellet Feed</li><li>Expanded Feed</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AquaFeed market types are divided into four categories: Mash Feed, Pellet Feed, Expanded Feed, and Other. Mash Feed refers to finely ground feed that does not undergo any further processing. Pellet Feed is produced by compressing the mash feed into pellets, providing better digestibility and reducing wastage. Expanded Feed is a type of pellet feed that is extruded under high temperatures and pressure, resulting in increased nutrition and digestibility. The Other category includes specialty feeds like crumble feed and gel feed, which are designed for specific aquatic species and feeding methods.</p></p>
