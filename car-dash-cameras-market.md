<p><p>The car dash cameras market consists of two main types: single lens type and dual lens type. Single lens type cameras have a single lens that captures footage of the road and surrounding environment in front of the car. They are designed to record the journey and act as evidence in case of accidents or disputes. On the other hand, dual lens type cameras have two lenses, one facing forward and the other facing the cabin. These cameras provide additional surveillance inside the vehicle, adding an extra layer of security and capturing both the road and the interior simultaneously.</p></p>
