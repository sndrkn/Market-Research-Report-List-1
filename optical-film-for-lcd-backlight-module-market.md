<p><p>The optical film market for LCD backlight modules consists of different types of films, including reflective film, diffusion film, and lighten film. Reflective film enhances the reflection of light, improving brightness and reducing power consumption. Diffusion film scatters light uniformly, reducing bright spots and enhancing viewing angles. Lighten film boosts the overall brightness of the display by controlling the amount of transmitted light. These films play a crucial role in enhancing the visual performance of LCD backlight modules by improving brightness, reducing glare, and ensuring uniform light distribution.</p></p>
