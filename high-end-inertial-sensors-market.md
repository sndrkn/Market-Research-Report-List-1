<p><p>The high-end inertial sensors market consists of various types of sensors used for precise measurement and navigation applications. Accelerometers measure acceleration and are commonly used in devices like smartphones and aircraft for motion detection. Gyroscopes measure angular velocity and are used in navigation systems and drones for stabilization and orientation. Magnetometers measure magnetic fields and find applications in compasses and electronic devices. GNSS receivers receive signals from global navigation satellite systems for accurate positioning and timing. Other types of inertial sensors may include pressure sensors, temperature sensors, and proximity sensors used in specific applications.</p></p>
