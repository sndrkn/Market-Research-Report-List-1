<p><p>Ophthalmic lens coating equipment refers to the machinery used in the application of coatings on eyeglass lenses. There are two main types: dip coating and spin coating. Dip coating involves immersing the lens into a coating material and then withdrawing it slowly, allowing the excess to drip off. Spin coating, on the other hand, involves placing the lens on a rotating spindle while a coating material is dispensed onto its surface, resulting in a uniform layer. Both methods are widely used in the ophthalmic lens industry for enhancing lens properties such as scratch resistance and glare reduction.</p></p>
