<p><ul><li>Water-borne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Reactive</li><li>Hot-melts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The weatherproof sealant market consists of different types, including water-borne, solvent-borne, reactive, and hot-melts. Water-borne sealants are composed of water as the primary solvent and are environmentally friendly. Solvent-borne sealants use organic solvents as their base and provide excellent adhesion. Reactive sealants are cured by a chemical reaction and offer high strength and durability. Hot-melts are solid at room temperature and require heating for application, providing fast adhesion and flexibility. Each type caters to specific requirements and is used in various industries for weatherproofing applications.</p></p>
