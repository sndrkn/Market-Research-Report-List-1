<p><p>The market for Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) Controllers can be categorized into four types: Series Controllers, Shunt Controllers, Shunt-Series Controllers, and Series-Series Controllers. Series Controllers are used to regulate voltage and power flow in transmission lines. Shunt Controllers control reactive power compensation at specific locations. Shunt-Series Controllers combine the functionalities of both Series and Shunt Controllers. Series-Series Controllers are used to enhance power transfer capacity between two transmission lines. These controllers play a crucial role in improving power system stability, transmission efficiency, and grid reliability.</p></p>
