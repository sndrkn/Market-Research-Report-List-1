<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Flexible steel rope is available in various market types, including Left Regular Lay, Left Lang Lay, Right Regular Lay, Right Lang Lay, and Alternate Lay. In Left Regular Lay, the strands are laid in a left-hand direction while the wires are laid in a right-hand direction. Similarly, in Left Lang Lay, the strands and wires are both laid in a left-hand direction. In Right Regular Lay, the strands and wires are laid in a right-hand direction, and in Right Lang Lay, the strands and wires are laid in a right-hand direction. Lastly, in Alternate Lay, the direction of the strands and wires alternate between left and right.</p></p>
