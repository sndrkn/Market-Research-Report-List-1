<p><p>The thermal interface materials market is segmented into various types. Thermal pads are soft, silicone-based materials used to fill gaps and provide thermal conductivity. Thermal grease and thermal paste are substances with high thermal conductivity that are applied between heat-generating components and cooling devices. Thermal adhesive is a substance used to permanently bond heat sinks to electronic components. Gap fillers are used to fill larger gaps, providing both thermal conductivity and mechanical support. Lastly, other types of thermal interface materials include tapes, films, and phase-change materials.</p></p>
