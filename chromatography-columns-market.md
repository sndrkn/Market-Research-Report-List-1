HPLC columns separate and analyze liquid samples, while GC columns are used for gas-phase separation. SPE columns are utilized for extracting specific compounds from a sample matrix. These columns are vital in various industries like pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and food and beverage for accurate analysis and purification of substances.
