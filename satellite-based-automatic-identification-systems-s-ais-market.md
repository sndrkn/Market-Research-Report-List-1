Class A Transponders are required for vessels above a certain size and transmit data more frequently, providing accurate and real-time information about the vessel's speed, location, and course. Class B Transponders, on the other hand, are more affordable and suitable for smaller vessels. They transmit data less frequently and have a shorter range but still provide essential information for tracking and safety purposes.</p></p>
