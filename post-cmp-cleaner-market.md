<p><p>Post CMP cleaners are used in the semiconductor industry to remove residual material after chemical mechanical polishing. There are two main types of Post CMP cleaners: acidic and alkaline. Acidic cleaners are formulated with acids such as sulfuric acid or hydrochloric acid to dissolve and remove metal residue. On the other hand, alkaline cleaners contain alkaline compounds like ammonia or potassium hydroxide to detach and eliminate organic or inorganic contaminants. The choice between acidic and alkaline cleaners depends on the specific type of material to be removed and the desired surface characteristics for subsequent processing.</p></p>
