<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The off-highway tire market can be categorized into three types: bias type, radial type, and solid type. Bias type tires are made of multiple layers of crisscrossed cords and provide high load-carrying capacity and durability. Radial type tires have reinforcing cords that run radially from bead to bead, offering better grip, stability, and fuel efficiency. Solid type tires are puncture-resistant, made of solid rubber and are suitable for rough terrains. These different types cater to various off-highway applications, considering factors like load capacity, traction, and durability.</p></p>
