The market analysis indicates that the increasing demand for coated papers from various end-use industries such as packaging, printing, and publishing is driving the market growth. The growing urbanization, rising disposable income, and increasing literacy rates are also contributing to the growth of the paper coating binders market.</p><p>In terms of trends, there is a rising popularity of environmentally friendly and sustainable paper coating binders.
