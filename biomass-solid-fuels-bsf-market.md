Straw, usually agricultural waste, can also be processed into pellets or briquettes for fuel. Rice husk is another abundant byproduct that can be utilized as fuel in biomass power plants or converted into briquettes. Other biomass sources, including crop residues and animal waste, can also be transformed into BSF, offering sustainable alternatives in the energy market.</p></p>
