It provides improved drug release profiles, enhanced tablet disintegration, and stability. It acts as a thickening agent, stabilizer, and emulsifier, and helps to improve the texture and stability of the products.</p><p>In the food processing industry, L-HPC is used as a thickening agent and stabilizer in a wide range of applications, including sauces, dressings, and dairy products.
