<p><p>Intelligent Sorting System Market Types include Linear Sortation Systems and Loop Sortation Systems. </p><p>Linear Sortation Systems refer to automated systems that transport items or goods in a linear manner, usually on a conveyor belt, allowing for efficient sorting based on predetermined criteria such as destination or product type.</p><p>Loop Sortation Systems, on the other hand, operate in a loop or circular fashion, creating a continuous flow of items. These systems are known for their high throughput and flexibility, as they can handle a large volume of items and easily adapt to changing sorting requirements.</p></p>
