<p><p>Hot forging lubricants are used to reduce friction and wear during the forging process, thereby enhancing the efficiency and quality of the forged products. Graphite-based forging lubricants contain graphite as a key ingredient, which provides excellent lubrication and high-temperature resistance. On the other hand, non-graphite-based forging lubricants do not contain graphite but rely on other additives to offer lubrication and protection. Both types of lubricants play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and efficient forging operations, but they differ in terms of their composition and performance characteristics.</p></p>
