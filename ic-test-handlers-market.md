<p><p>IC Test Handlers are machines used in the semiconductor industry to automate the testing process of integrated circuits (ICs). There are three main types of IC Test Handlers: </p><p>1. Pick-And-Place Handlers: These handlers use robotic arms to pick ICs from one location and place them onto test sockets. They are precise and ideal for high-speed testing.</p><p>2. Gravity-Feed Handlers: These handlers rely on gravity to feed ICs into test sockets. They are simpler in design and suitable for lower-volume testing.</p><p>3. Turret Handlers: These handlers have rotating turrets with multiple test sockets, allowing for simultaneous testing of multiple ICs. They offer high throughput and are commonly used for mass testing purposes.</p></p>
