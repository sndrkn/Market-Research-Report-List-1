<p><p>Organic linen market consists of three main types of yarn: dry spun, wet spun, and blended. Dry spun yarn is made by spinning fibers without any moisture, resulting in a crisp and sturdy yarn suitable for upholstery and home furnishings. Wet spun yarn involves wetting fibers during the spinning process, creating a soft and smooth yarn ideal for clothing and textiles. Blended yarn combines organic linen with other fibers like cotton or silk, offering a unique combination of qualities for various applications in the market.</p></p>
