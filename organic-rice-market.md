<p><p>The organic rice market offers three main types: polished glutinous rice, Indica (long-shaped rice), and polished round-grained rice. Polished glutinous rice, also known as sticky rice, is a staple in many Asian cuisines and is favored for its sticky texture. Indica rice is characterized by its long grains and is commonly used for biryanis and pilaf dishes. Finally, polished round-grained rice, often used in sushi and risotto, has a shorter grain and a slightly stickier texture compared to Indica rice. All three types are available in the organic rice market.</p></p>
