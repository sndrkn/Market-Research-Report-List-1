<p><p>The lithium-ion battery electrolyte additives market comprises several types, including film-forming additives, flame retardant additives, high and low temperature additives, and others. Film-forming additives improve the formation and stability of solid electrolyte interface on the battery's electrode surface. Flame retardant additives ensure the safety of the battery by reducing the risk of combustion or explosion. High and low temperature additives enhance battery performance in extreme temperature conditions. Other additives include conductive additives, oxygen scavengers, and stabilizers that improve battery efficiency, lifespan, and overall performance.</p></p>
