<p><p>Diffusion pump fluids are used in vacuum systems to generate a high vacuum by creating a vapor cloud that displaces gas molecules. Silicone diffusion pump fluids are widely used due to their excellent pumping speed and thermal stability. They are suitable for general-purpose vacuum applications. On the other hand, polyphenylene ether diffusion pump fluids offer superior performance in high vacuum systems with low vapor pressures. They are often utilized in applications where extremely low ultimate pressures are required, such as in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
