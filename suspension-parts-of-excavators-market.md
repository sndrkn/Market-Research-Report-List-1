<p><strong>The Suspension Parts of Excavators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Connected Type</li><li>Semi-Floating</li><li>Full Floating</li><li>Independent Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Suspension parts in the excavators market can be categorized into different types based on their connection and mounting method. Rigid connected type refers to a suspension system where the parts are directly connected without any flexibility. Semi-floating suspension allows for some movement and flexibility in the connection, providing a partial suspension effect. Full floating suspension offers complete flexibility and movement in the connection, allowing for improved shock absorption. Independent mounted suspension means that each part is mounted separately, providing a higher level of flexibility and independent movement in the suspension system.</p></p>
