Factors such as increasing interest in wildlife photography, growing popularity of hunting as a recreational activity, and the need for wildlife conservation are driving the demand for these cameras. Hunting game and trail cameras are increasingly being integrated with smartphones and other portable devices, allowing users to view live feeds, control camera settings, and access stored data remotely.
