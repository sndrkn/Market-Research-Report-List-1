<p><p>The rigid inflatable boat (RIB) market can be categorized into two types: double tube boat RIBs and single tube boat RIBs. Double tube boat RIBs are designed with two inflatable tubes on either side of the boat, offering enhanced stability and buoyancy. They are commonly used in rough waters and are favored by rescue teams and military operations. In contrast, single tube boat RIBs feature a single inflatable tube, providing better maneuverability and speed. They are often used for recreational purposes and in calmer waters.</p></p>
