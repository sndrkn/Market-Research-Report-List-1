<p><p>Non-woven wheels are available in various types based on the materials used in their construction. One common type is made of PA (polyamide) material, which provides high resilience and is suitable for heavy-duty applications. PP (polypropylene) wheels offer good load-bearing capacity and resistance to chemicals and moisture. PE (polyethylene) wheels are known for their durability, low friction, and resistance to wear and tear. Lastly, there are non-woven wheels made from other materials that cater to specific requirements, such as higher heat resistance or enhanced cleansing properties. These different types serve different industrial needs, ensuring a wide range of options for potential buyers.</p></p>
