<p><p>The custom blend food color market offers three main types: powder, liquid, and gel. Powder food colors are dry and can easily be mixed with other powdered ingredients. They provide a more concentrated and vibrant color effect. Liquid food colors come in the form of liquid drops and are best suited for recipes with a high moisture content, such as beverages. They are also easier to blend and distribute evenly. Gel food colors have a thicker consistency, making them ideal for coloring icings and frostings, as they do not dilute the mixture.</p></p>
