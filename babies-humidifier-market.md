<p><p>The market for baby humidifiers can be classified into two types: warm mist and cool mist. Warm mist humidifiers release steam into the air, created by heating water. These humidifiers are known for providing soothing relief for congestion and cough. Cool mist humidifiers, on the other hand, disperse a cool mist into the room by using a fan or ultrasonic technology. They are often preferred for their safety and ability to humidify larger areas. Both types of humidifiers help to maintain optimal air moisture levels in the room, promoting better sleep and respiratory health for babies.</p></p>
