<p><p>The Trace Metal Analysis Instrument Market includes various types of instruments for analyzing trace metals in different samples. These instruments are Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy, X-Ray Fluorescence, Inductively Coupled Plasma Mass Spectrometry (ICP-MS), and others. Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy measures the absorption of light by atoms to detect and quantify trace metals. X-Ray Fluorescence uses X-rays to excite atoms and measures the emitted fluorescent radiation for analysis. ICP-MS combines mass spectrometry with inductively coupled plasma to measure the concentration of trace metals. Other instruments may include electrochemical methods or other spectroscopic techniques.</p></p>
