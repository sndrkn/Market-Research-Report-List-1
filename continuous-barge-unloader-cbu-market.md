<p><strong>The Continuous Barge Unloader (CBU) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bridge Type</li><li>Cantilever Type</li><li>Elevating Type</li><li>Traveling Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Barge Unloader (CBU) market consists of four main types: Bridge Type, Cantilever Type, Elevating Type, and Traveling Type. Bridge Type CBUs are mounted on a fixed structure and typically span across the width of the barge, making them suitable for unloading barges of various sizes. Cantilever Type CBUs have a support structure at one end, allowing for flexible positioning and efficient unloading operations. Elevating Type CBUs have a vertical height-adjustment mechanism that enables them to handle barges with different water levels. Traveling Type CBUs can move horizontally along a rail system, providing flexibility for unloading different areas in a terminal.</p></p>
