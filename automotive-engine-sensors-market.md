<p><ul><li>Process Sensors</li><li>Position Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive engine sensors market consists of two main types: process sensors and position sensors. Process sensors are used to monitor and measure various factors related to the engine's functions, such as temperature, pressure, oxygen levels, and flow rate. On the other hand, position sensors are responsible for detecting the position or movement of engine components, such as crankshaft position, throttle position, and camshaft position. These sensors play a crucial role in providing real-time data to the engine control unit, enabling efficient engine performance and improving overall vehicle reliability.</p></p>
