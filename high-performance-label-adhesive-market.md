<p><p>The high performance label adhesive market is divided into two types: water-based label adhesive and solvent-based label adhesive. Water-based label adhesive is made up of water as the main solvent and is more environmentally friendly. It offers good adhesion and is commonly used in the food and beverage industry. On the other hand, solvent-based label adhesive uses organic solvents and offers excellent adhesion properties. It is often used in applications where high temperature or chemical resistance is required, such as in the automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
