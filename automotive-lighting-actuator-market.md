<p><p>The Automotive Lighting Actuator market can be categorized into three types: Halogen, Xenon, and LED. Halogen lights, being the most common, produce light by heating a tungsten filament. Xenon lights, also known as HID lights, use xenon gas to generate a brighter and whiter light. LED lights, known for their energy efficiency, utilize light-emitting diodes to produce light. Each lighting type offers different advantages and characteristics, making them suitable for various automotive lighting applications.</p></p>
