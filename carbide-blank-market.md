<p><p>The carbide blank market consists of two main types: Coarse Grain WC and Fine Grain WC. Each market segment caters to specific industrial applications, with Coarse Grain WC being optimal for heavy-duty cutting and Fine Grain WC better suited for precision machining.</p></p>
