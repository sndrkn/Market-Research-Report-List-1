<p><p>The hands-free devices market is segregated into two types: wireless and wired. Wireless hands-free devices rely on Bluetooth technology to establish a connection between the device and the user's smartphone or another compatible device. These devices offer freedom of movement as they eliminate the need for physical wires. On the other hand, wired hands-free devices utilize physical connections, typically through auxiliary ports or USB cables, to establish a connection. These devices are known for their reliability and uninterrupted audio quality due to the direct wired connection.</p></p>
