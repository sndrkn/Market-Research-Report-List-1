<p><p>The Fiberglass Meshes market comprises two main types: Interior Fiberglass Mesh and External Fiberglass Mesh. Interior Fiberglass Mesh is primarily used for reinforcing plaster walls and ceilings in indoor areas. It helps in preventing cracking and improves overall durability. On the other hand, External Fiberglass Mesh is designed to provide reinforcement and weather resistance to external walls and facades. It helps in maintaining the structural integrity of the building and protecting it from external elements like wind, rain, and extreme weather conditions. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the strength and longevity of construction projects.</p></p>
