<p><p>Dyeing auxiliaries are essential components used in the dyeing process of textiles. Dispersing agents help disperse and stabilize dyes in the dye bath, ensuring even and uniform coloration. Sequestering agents prevent the formation of metal ions, which can affect the dyeing process. Scouring agents eliminate impurities and prepare the fabric for dyeing. Wetting agents enable better dye penetration and dispersion on the fabric surface. Other dyeing auxiliaries include leveling agents, anti-foaming agents, and pH regulators that assist in obtaining desired dyeing results, such as improved color fastness and increased dye uptake.</p></p>
