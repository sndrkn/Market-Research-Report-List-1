<p><strong>The CMP Abrasive Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silica-Based Type</li><li>Ceria-Based Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) abrasive materials are used in the semiconductor industry for polishing and planarizing surfaces. Silica-based CMP abrasives are made from silica particles and are commonly used for polishing silicon wafers due to their excellent planarization and selectivity properties. On the other hand, ceria-based CMP abrasives utilize cerium oxide particles and are suitable for polishing various materials, including metals and dielectrics, due to their high removal rate and low defectivity. Both types of CMP abrasive materials play a crucial role in achieving precise and smooth surfaces in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
