<p><ul><li>Lap Type</li><li>Socket Type</li><li>Compound Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PU Insulation Board refers to a type of insulation material made from polyurethane foam. There are three main types of PU Insulation Board on the market. </p><p>Firstly, the Lap Type is characterized by overlapping edges that allow for a more secure and reliable installation process. </p><p>Secondly, the Socket Type has interlocking edges that create a tight seal, enhancing the insulation board's overall efficiency.</p><p>Lastly, the Compound Type combines the features of the Lap and Socket Types, providing both overlapping edges and interlocking edges for optimal insulation performance. These different types offer customers various choices depending on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
