<p><p>The brackish water membranes market consists of various types of membranes used for desalination or purification of brackish water sources. Reverse Osmosis (RO) membranes are commonly used for high salt rejection, while Nanofiltration (NF) membranes are suitable for medium to low salt rejection. Forward Osmosis (FO) membranes excel in low-pressure applications and require a draw solution. Electrodialysis (ED) membranes separate salts using an electrical current. These membranes play a crucial role in addressing water scarcity issues and providing clean water solutions globally.</p></p>
