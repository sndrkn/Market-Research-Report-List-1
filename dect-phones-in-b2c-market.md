<p><p>DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) phones in the B2C market come in two primary types: SIP DECT and IP DECT. SIP DECT phones use the Session Initiation Protocol (SIP) to transmit voice data over an internet connection, allowing users to make calls via the internet. IP DECT phones, on the other hand, connect to an IP (Internet Protocol) network directly, without the need for a separate base station. Both types of DECT phones offer wireless communication and are popular choices for residential consumers seeking reliable and flexible telephony options.</p></p>
