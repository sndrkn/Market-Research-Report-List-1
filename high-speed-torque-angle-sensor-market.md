<p><ul><li>Potentiometer Torque Sensor</li><li>Non-contact Torque Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Speed Torque Angle Sensor market includes two main types of sensors: Potentiometer Torque Sensors and Non-contact Torque Sensors. Potentiometer Torque Sensors measure torque by using a resistive element that changes its position based on the applied torque. On the other hand, Non-contact Torque Sensors utilize advanced technologies such as magnetoelasticity or magnetic fields to measure torque without the need for physical contact. These sensors are commonly used in various industries to accurately measure and monitor torque in high-speed applications.</p></p>
