<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Hollow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Stabilizer Market includes two types of stabilizers: Solid and Hollow. Solid stabilizers are compact and made from a single material without any cavities inside. They offer high rigidity and stability, ensuring effective vehicle control and minimizing body roll during cornering or abrupt maneuvers. On the other hand, Hollow stabilizers have an empty space inside, resulting in reduced weight. They provide enhanced flexibility, allowing smoother handling and a more comfortable ride. Both solid and hollow stabilizers play a crucial role in enhancing the overall performance and safety of new energy vehicles.</p></p>
