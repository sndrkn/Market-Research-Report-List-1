<p><p>The Automotive Diesel Common Rail Injection System market is categorized into two types: Solenoid Type and Piezo Type. Solenoid Type injection systems use solenoid valves to control the fuel injection process, ensuring precise fuel delivery. Piezo Type injection systems, on the other hand, utilize piezoelectric crystals to control fuel injection, enabling faster and more accurate fuel injection timings. These systems play a crucial role in improving fuel efficiency, reducing emissions, and enhancing engine performance in modern diesel vehicles.</p></p>
