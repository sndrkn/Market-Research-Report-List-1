The Instrument Landing System (ILS) is a widely used ground-based technology that provides precise guidance to pilots. Ground-Based Augmentation System (GBAS) is another technology that enhances the accuracy of GPS signals for approach and landing. The Microwave Landing System (MLS) is an alternative to ILS, utilizing microwave frequencies to provide precise positioning and guidance to pilots. These technologies significantly improve flight safety and operational efficiency in challenging weather scenarios.</p></p>
