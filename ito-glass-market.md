<p><p>ITO Glass is widely used in various industries, and it can be categorized into two types: Low Resistance ITO Glass and High Resistance ITO Glass. </p><p>Low Resistance ITO Glass has a more conductive surface, making it suitable for applications requiring high transmission and low sheet resistance, like touch screens and LCD displays. On the other hand, High Resistance ITO Glass has a relatively higher sheet resistance, making it optimal for applications that require electrical insulation, such as solar cells and electronic devices. These market types cater to different industry needs, offering specific advantages based on their conductive properties.</p></p>
