<p><p>The Embedded Integrated Circuit (IC) market encompasses various types of electronic components. Firstly, a Single Chip Microcomputer refers to a compact IC that contains all the essential features of a computer system, including a processor, memory, and peripherals, on a single chip. Secondly, a Micro-Controller Unit (MCU) is an IC that integrates a processor, memory, and input/output interfaces, specifically designed for embedded systems. Lastly, the "other" market in the Embedded IC market includes different types of ICs such as Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), Digital Signal Processors (DSPs), and Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs), catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
