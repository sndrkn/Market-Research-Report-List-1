<p><p>The Waterproof Salinity Meters market can be classified into two types: High Range and Low Range. High Range salinity meters are designed to measure salt concentration in water samples with high salt content, such as brine solutions or ocean water. These meters are capable of measuring salinity levels up to a certain threshold typically expressed in parts per thousand (ppt). In contrast, Low Range salinity meters are used to measure salt concentration in water samples with lower salt content, such as freshwater or mildly saline water. They have a lower measurement threshold than High Range meters.</p></p>
