<p><p>Polycarbonates (PC) Films market is categorized into two primary types: Type I and Type II. Type I films are known for their high transparency, impact resistance, and excellent heat resistance properties. These films find extensive applications in industries such as automotive, electronics, and optical lenses. On the other hand, Type II films exhibit good flame retardancy along with other properties like impact resistance and heat resistance. Type II films are commonly used in applications that demand higher fire safety standards, such as construction, electrical, and aerospace industries.</p></p>
