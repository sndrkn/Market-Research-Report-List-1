<p><p>Tooth positioners are devices used in orthodontics to correct the alignment of teeth. There are two main types of tooth positioners: non-extraction positioners and extraction positioners. Non-extraction positioners are used when there is enough space in the mouth to realign the teeth without the need for removing any teeth. On the other hand, extraction positioners are used when there is overcrowding and certain teeth need to be removed to create space for proper alignment. Both types of positioners aim to achieve optimal dental alignment and improve overall oral health.</p></p>
