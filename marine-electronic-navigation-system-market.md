<p><strong>The Marine Electronic Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vector Chart (ENC)</li><li>Raster Chart (RNC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine electronic navigation system market includes two types of charts: vector charts (ENC) and raster charts (RNC). ENC charts are digital charts that use vectors to represent geographical features, allowing for easy updating and customization. They offer detailed information, accurate positioning, and are preferred by commercial vessels. On the other hand, RNC charts are scanned images of paper charts and provide a more familiar view. They are often used by recreational boaters as they offer simplicity and ease of use.</p></p>
