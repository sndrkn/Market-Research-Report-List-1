<p><p>Medical Grade PolyStyrene (MPS) is a type of plastic material used in the medical industry. It is available in different forms, including General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS), High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS), and Expandable Polystyrene (EPS). GPPS is commonly used in sterile packaging, medical devices, and labware due to its transparency and excellent dimensional stability. HIPS, with its superior impact resistance, is suitable for medical equipment housing and instrument trays. EPS, known for its lightweight and insulation properties, finds application in medical coolers and transportation of temperature-sensitive medical products like vaccines and organs.</p></p>
