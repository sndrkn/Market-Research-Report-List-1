<p><p>The Aircraft Struts market comprises three types: Rigid Aircraft Struts, Spring Steel Aircraft Struts, and Shock Struts. Rigid Aircraft Struts are fixed structures that provide support and stability to the aircraft during landing and takeoff. Spring Steel Aircraft Struts use steel springs to absorb shock and reduce the impact on the aircraft's structure. Shock Struts utilize hydraulic or pneumatic systems to absorb and dampen the energy generated during landing, ensuring a smooth and controlled touchdown. These different types cater to various aircraft requirements and contribute to safe and efficient operations.</p></p>
