<p><p>The fully electric CNC pipe bender market includes two main types: single-head and twin-head. In a single-head CNC pipe bender, only one bending head is present, which allows for bending operations on a single pipe at a time. On the other hand, a twin-head CNC pipe bender has two bending heads, enabling simultaneous bending on two separate pipes. These different market types cater to the varying needs and production capacities of different industries and applications requiring precision pipe bending.</p></p>
