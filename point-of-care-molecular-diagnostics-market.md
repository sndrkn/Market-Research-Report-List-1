<p><p>Point-of-care molecular diagnostics market includes various types of technologies used for rapid and accurate diagnosis of diseases at the patient's bedside. PCR-based technologies involve the amplification of genetic material to detect specific DNA sequences. Genetic sequencing-based methods determine the entire genetic code of an organism to identify potential mutations. Hybridization-based techniques involve the detection of target nucleic acids by molecular probes binding to complementary sequences. Microarray-based methods use small microscopic arrays to simultaneously analyze multiple genes or molecular targets, enabling quick diagnosis of various diseases.</p></p>
