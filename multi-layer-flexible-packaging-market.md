<p><strong>The Multi-layer Flexible Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top Loading</li><li>Bottom Loading</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-layer flexible packaging market comprises two types: top loading and bottom loading. In top loading packaging, the opening of the package is at the top, allowing easy access for loading and unloading of products. This type is commonly used for items like snacks, pet food, and powdered goods. On the other hand, bottom loading packaging has the opening at the bottom, enabling products to be dispensed from the package's lower end. This design is often applied to liquids and granular substances, as it simplifies pouring or emptying the contents.</p></p>
