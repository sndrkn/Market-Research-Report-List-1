<p><p>A hiatal hernia graft refers to a surgical procedure that involves repairing a hiatal hernia. Hiatal hernia occurs when a portion of the stomach protrudes through the diaphragm into the chest cavity, leading to symptoms such as acid reflux, heartburn, and difficulty swallowing. The graft procedure aims to strengthen the hiatus (natural opening in the diaphragm) and prevent the stomach from bulging through it.</p><p>The global hiatal hernia graft market is projected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. The market analysis suggests that the increasing prevalence of hiatal hernia, along with the growing aging population, is driving the market growth. Factors such as obesity, improper posture, and weakening of diaphragmatic muscles contribute to the development of hiatal hernia. Thus, the rise in these conditions is expected to boost the demand for hiatal hernia graft surgeries.</p><p>Technological advancements and innovations in surgical procedures also play a crucial role in the market growth.
