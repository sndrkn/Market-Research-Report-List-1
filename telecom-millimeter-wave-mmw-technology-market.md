E-Band, operating between 60 and 90 GHz, offers higher data rates and is mainly employed for backhaul in wireless communication systems. </p><p>Other Frequency Bands typically include frequencies above 90 GHz and are used for specific applications like military, scientific research, and imaging systems. These different market types cater to various communication needs, offering unique advantages and uses in the telecom industry.</p></p>
