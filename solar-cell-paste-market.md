<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The solar cell paste market consists of three main types: Al paste, Ag (Front) paste, and Ag (Back) paste. Al paste is used as a rear contact material in solar cells, ensuring efficient electrical conductivity and lower contact resistance. Ag (Front) paste is applied on the front side of solar cells to create grid patterns for effective current collection. Ag (Back) paste is used on the back side to form a reflective layer, reducing the loss of light absorbed by the solar cell. These pastes play essential roles in maximizing solar cell efficiency and performance.</p></p>
