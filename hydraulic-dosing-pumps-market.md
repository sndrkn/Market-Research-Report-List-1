<p><p>Hydraulic dosing pumps are used to accurately inject fluids in various industrial applications. Diaphragm pumps are a type of hydraulic dosing pump that use a flexible diaphragm to push the fluid from the dosing chamber. They are known for their compact size, low maintenance, and suitability for low-pressure applications. On the other hand, piston pumps, another type of hydraulic dosing pump, use a reciprocating piston to move the fluid. They are preferred for high-pressure applications and offer greater accuracy and control. Both types are reliable and efficient solutions for precise fluid dosing in different industries.</p></p>
