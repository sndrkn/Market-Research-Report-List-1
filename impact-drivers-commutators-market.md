Hook type commutators have a hook-shaped design that allows for easy installation and removal. Groove commutators have grooves that accommodate the brushes and help improve electrical contact. These variations provide options for manufacturers to choose the most suitable commutator for their impact drivers' electrical requirements.</p></p>
