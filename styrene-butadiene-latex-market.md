<p><p>The Styrene Butadiene Latex market includes two main types: Emulsion Styrene Butadiene Latex and Solution Styrene Butadiene Latex. Emulsion Styrene Butadiene Latex is a water-based product made by emulsifying styrene and butadiene polymers. It finds applications in various industries due to its excellent adhesive and film-forming properties. Solution Styrene Butadiene Latex, on the other hand, is a non-aqueous product produced by dissolving styrene and butadiene polymers in a solvent. It is widely used in applications where water resistance is required, such as coatings and adhesives.</p></p>
