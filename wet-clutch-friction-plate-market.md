<p><p>The wet clutch friction plate market is categorized into four types: AT (Automatic Transmission), DCT (Dual Clutch Transmission), CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission), and Others. AT refers to the type of transmission that automatically changes gear ratios as the vehicle accelerates or decelerates. DCT is a type of transmission that utilizes two separate clutches for odd and even gears, providing faster gear shifts. CVT is a type of transmission that enables smooth and continuous gear ratio changes without distinct gear shifts. The "Others" category includes different types of transmissions not mentioned above, such as manual or semi-automatic.</p></p>
