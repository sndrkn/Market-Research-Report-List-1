<p><p>The Zirconia Dental Product market consists of two main types: Zirconia Dental Disc and Zirconia Dental Block. A Zirconia Dental Disc is a circular material that is commonly used for fabricating dental restorations like crowns and bridges. On the other hand, a Zirconia Dental Block is a solid block of zirconia that is typically used in computer-aided milling machines to produce dental prostheses. Both these products serve as high-quality alternatives to traditional materials, offering durability, biocompatibility, and aesthetic appeal in the field of dentistry.</p></p>
