<p><p>The floor-mounted fume hood market offers three main types: top exhaust type, bottom exhaust type, and top and bottom exhaust type. The top exhaust type fume hoods are designed to extract and expel harmful fumes through the top of the hood, ensuring a safer working environment. Conversely, the bottom exhaust type fume hoods evacuate fumes through the bottom, effectively capturing and removing contaminants. Lastly, the top and bottom exhaust type fume hoods combine the features of both previously mentioned types, providing a comprehensive solution for fume extraction and containment.</p></p>
