<p><strong>The Medical Ultrasonic Cutter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Ultrasonic Cutter market consists of two types: contact and non-contact. Contact ultrasonic cutters involve direct physical contact with the object being cut, providing precise and efficient results. They are commonly used in surgical procedures for tissue dissection and removal. On the other hand, non-contact ultrasonic cutters function without touching the object, utilizing focused ultrasound waves for cutting. These types are suitable for delicate or heat-sensitive materials, finding applications in industries like electronics and food processing. Both contact and non-contact ultrasonic cutters serve different purposes and offer unique advantages based on their specific applications.</p></p>
