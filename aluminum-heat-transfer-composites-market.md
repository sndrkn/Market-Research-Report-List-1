<p><p>The aluminum heat transfer composites market can be classified into two main types: high-aluminum and low-aluminum. High-aluminum composites have a higher concentration of aluminum, which provides excellent thermal conductivity and heat transfer properties. These composites are often used in industries where efficient heat dissipation is crucial. On the other hand, low-aluminum composites have a lower aluminum content, making them more cost-effective but with slightly lower heat transfer capabilities. They are commonly used in applications where cost is a significant factor, but high thermal conductivity is not critical.</p></p>
