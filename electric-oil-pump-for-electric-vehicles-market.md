There are two types of electric oil pumps available in the market: integrated electric oil pump and separate electric oil pump. An integrated electric oil pump is installed inside the engine, making it compact and lightweight. On the other hand, a separate electric oil pump is located outside the engine, providing flexibility in installation and maintenance. Both types play a vital role in ensuring the smooth operation of EVs.</p></p>
