<p><ul><li>Ablative Laser</li><li>Non-Ablative Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for lasers in cosmetic surgery can be categorized into two types: ablative laser and non-ablative laser. Ablative lasers are used to remove the outer layer of the skin, stimulating the production of collagen and promoting skin rejuvenation. They are commonly used for treating wrinkles, scars, and pigmentation issues. On the other hand, non-ablative lasers penetrate the skin without removing any layers, delivering heat to stimulate collagen production and improve skin texture. Non-ablative lasers are less invasive, making them ideal for treating mild skin conditions.</p></p>
