<p><ul><li>Metalized Barrier Films</li><li>Transparent Barrier Films</li><li>White Barrier Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical packaging barrier film market can be categorized into three types: metalized barrier films, transparent barrier films, and white barrier films. Metalized barrier films provide high barrier properties and protect medical products from light and oxygen. Transparent barrier films offer excellent clarity, allowing visibility of the product without compromising its protection against moisture and oxygen. White barrier films offer both barrier properties and opacity, ensuring protection against light, moisture, and oxygen. These different types of barrier films cater to the diverse packaging requirements of the medical industry, providing the necessary protection and preservation for medical products.</p></p>
