<p><p>A Multi-Chamber Sputtering System is a type of system used for depositing thin films of materials onto substrates. The Load-lock type market refers to systems that have a dedicated chamber for loading and unloading substrates, providing a clean and controlled environment. This type is suitable for high-throughput applications. On the other hand, the Batch Type market refers to systems that can process multiple substrates simultaneously in a single chamber. This type is more suitable for research and development or low-volume production applications. Both types offer different advantages based on the specific requirements of the users.</p></p>
