<p><p>Single-pane dive masks come in two market types: full and half masks. Full masks cover the entire face, providing a wider field of view underwater and allowing for easier equalization. They are commonly used for scuba diving and provide better leak protection. On the other hand, half masks only cover the eyes and nose, which allow for easier access to other equipment like regulators or snorkels. They are generally favored by snorkelers or free divers who require less equipment and prefer a more unobstructed view.</p></p>
