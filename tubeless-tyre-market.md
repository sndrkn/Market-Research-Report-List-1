<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The tubeless tyre market consists of two main types: radial tubeless tyres and bias tubeless tyres. Radial tubeless tyres are constructed with radial plies that provide better grip, stability, and heat resistance. They are commonly used in passenger cars and high-performance vehicles. On the other hand, bias tubeless tyres feature diagonal plies that offer a stiffer sidewall and higher load-carrying capacity. These tyres are often utilized in off-road vehicles, agricultural machinery, and heavy-duty applications. Both types aim to eliminate the need for an inner tube, providing improved safety and convenience for the users.</p></p>
