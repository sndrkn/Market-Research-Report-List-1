<p><p>The airport sweepers market is categorized into four types - Ride-On Type, Truck-Mounted Type, Walk-Behind Type, and Others. Ride-On Type sweepers are large machines that are operated by a person sitting on them and are suitable for cleaning larger areas. Truck-Mounted Type sweepers are mounted on trucks and are used for cleaning wider and longer runways. Walk-Behind Type sweepers are smaller machines that are pushed and operated by a person walking behind them, making them ideal for narrow areas. The "Others" category includes any other types of sweepers not fitting into the above three categories.</p></p>
