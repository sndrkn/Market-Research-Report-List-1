<p><p>The Alcohol Breath Analyzer market consists of three main types - Fuel Cell, Semiconductor, and Infrared. Fuel Cell technology utilizes a chemical reaction to produce an electric current as the breath sample interacts with the electrodes. Semiconductor sensors rely on the change in electrical resistance when exposed to alcohol vapors. Infrared sensors detect alcohol levels by analyzing the absorption of infrared light. Each type offers different levels of accuracy, sensitivity, and reliability in measuring alcohol content. These technologies play a crucial role in ensuring public safety by detecting alcohol impairment.</p></p>
