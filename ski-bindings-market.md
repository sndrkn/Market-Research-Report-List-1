<p><p>The ski bindings market is divided into three main types: strap-in, step-in, and other. Strap-in bindings require the skier to manually fasten the straps around their ski boots, providing a secure fit. Step-in bindings allow skiers to easily slide their boots into the bindings and lock them in place, offering convenience and speed. The "other" market category includes various binding types, such as specialty or hybrid designs that may incorporate elements of both strap-in and step-in bindings or offer unique features that cater to specific skier preferences.</p></p>
