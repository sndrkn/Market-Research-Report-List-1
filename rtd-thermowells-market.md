<p><p>RTD Thermowells are used to protect temperature sensors in various industrial applications. Threaded Thermowells are cylindrical tubes with threads on the outside that can be screwed into a threaded opening, providing easy installation and removal. Flanged Thermowells have flanges on one end, allowing them to be bolted onto a process pipe or vessel. Socket Weld Thermowells have a socket for welding onto a pipe, providing a secure and leak-tight connection. Other types may include sanitary thermowells, weld-in thermowells, or thermowells with special coatings or materials. These various types cater to different installation and operational requirements.</p></p>
