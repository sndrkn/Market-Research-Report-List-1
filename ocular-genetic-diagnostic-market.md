<p><p>Ocular Genetic Diagnostic Market Types refers to the different technologies used in the field of genetic diagnostics for ocular diseases. Next-generation Sequencing (NGS) is a high-throughput method that can analyze multiple genes simultaneously and provide detailed information about genetic variations. Multiplex Ligation-Dependent Probe Amplification (MLPA) is a technique that can detect genetic deletions or duplications in targeted genes. Fragment Analysis PCR for Repeat Expansion Analysis is used to identify expansions of repetitive DNA sequences, which are associated with certain ocular disorders.</p></p>
