<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The dual clutch transmission (DCT) market is categorized into two types: wet dual clutch technology and dry dual clutch technology. Wet DCT utilizes a system where the clutch is immersed in a lubricating fluid, offering better heat dissipation and increased torque capacity. On the other hand, dry DCT does not require a lubricating fluid and relies on friction materials instead. Both technologies have their advantages and applications, with wet DCT being suitable for higher torque applications, while dry DCT offers better fuel efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
