<p><p>The Corrugated Gasket market comprises two main types: Semi-Metallic Material and Metallic Material. </p><p>Semi-Metallic Material gaskets are made of a combination of non-metallic materials, such as graphite or PTFE, with metallic components. These gaskets offer excellent resistance to high temperatures, corrosion, and pressure, making them suitable for various applications in industries like oil and gas, chemical, and power generation.</p><p>Metallic Material gaskets, on the other hand, are made entirely of metal, such as stainless steel or copper. These gaskets are highly durable, offering superior sealing performance, making them ideal for applications involving high-pressure and extreme temperatures, commonly found in the oil and gas, petrochemical, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
