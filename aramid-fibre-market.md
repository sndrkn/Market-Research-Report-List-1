<p><p>Aramid fiber is a high-performance synthetic fiber that is widely used in various industries for its exceptional strength and heat resistance properties. The aramid fiber market can be categorized into three types: para-aramid, meta-aramid, and others. Para-aramid fibers, such as Kevlar, exhibit excellent tensile strength and are commonly utilized in ballistic protection and aerospace applications. Meta-aramid fibers, such as Nomex, offer outstanding thermal stability and are primarily used in thermal protective apparel and electrical insulation. The "others" category comprises of blended aramid fibers and derivatives that find applications in diverse sectors like automotive, filtration, and construction.</p></p>
