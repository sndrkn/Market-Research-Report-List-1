<p><p>The Melamine Molding Compound market consists of two major types: Injection Molding and Compression Molding. In Injection Molding, melamine is heated and then injected into a mold cavity under high pressure. This process allows for the production of complex shapes, intricate details, and high volumes. Compression Molding involves placing preheated melamine material into a heated mold cavity. Pressure is then applied to compress the material into the desired shape. This method is suitable for larger, thicker, and more durable products. Both methods provide versatility and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
