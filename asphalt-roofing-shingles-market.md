<p><p>The asphalt roofing shingles market is divided into two main types: laminated shingles and three-tab shingles. Laminated shingles, also known as architectural or dimensional shingles, have multiple layers of asphalt and are designed to resemble the appearance of natural materials like wood or slate. They offer a more aesthetic appeal and have a longer lifespan. On the other hand, three-tab shingles have a more simplistic design with three uniform tabs, making them more cost-effective and easier to install. Both types of shingles serve as popular choices for residential roofing due to their durability and versatility.</p></p>
