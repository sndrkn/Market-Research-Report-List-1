The market for these stents can be categorized into two types: Polymer-based Stents and Metallic Stents. Polymer-based Stents are made from a biocompatible material that absorbs into the body, while Metallic Stents are made from metals like stainless steel or cobalt chromium, and provide temporary support to the artery.
