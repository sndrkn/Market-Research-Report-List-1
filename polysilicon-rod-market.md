<p><ul><li>Silane Method</li><li>Metallurgical Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polysilicon rod market consists of two main types: the Silane Method and the Metallurgical Process. In the Silane Method, high-purity polysilicon is produced through the chemical reaction of silane gas. This method offers advantages like high efficiency and low energy consumption. On the other hand, the Metallurgical Process involves the extraction of low-purity polysilicon from metallurgical-grade silicon, making it relatively less efficient but more cost-effective. Both methods cater to the growing demand for polysilicon, which is widely used in the production of solar cells and electronic devices.</p></p>
