<p><p>Modular construction elements refer to pre-fabricated components that can be easily assembled and disassembled to create various structures. These elements are typically categorized into different types such as diaphragms, curtains, profiles, booths, and others. Diaphragms are load-bearing panels used for structural support, while curtains are used for dividing spaces. Profiles are components that provide structural integrity and connect different elements together. Booths are enclosed structures used for various purposes. Others refer to any additional modular elements that do not fall into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
