<p><p>The automotive sensor interconnection market consists of different types such as single-hole, two-holes, porous, and others. Single-hole interconnections have a single opening for sensor insertion, allowing for easy installation and replacement. Two-hole interconnections have two openings for sensors, enabling the use of multiple sensors in a single interconnection system. Porous interconnections have small openings throughout their structure, providing a larger surface area and enhanced sensitivity. Other types of interconnections include customized designs, multi-channel systems, and wireless connections, catering to specific requirements in the automotive sensor industry.</p></p>
