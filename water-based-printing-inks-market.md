<p><p>The water-based printing inks market consists of three key types: flexo inks, gravure inks, and screen printing inks. Flexo inks are primarily used for printing on flexible substrates, such as paper, cardboard, and plastic. Gravure inks are employed in the gravure printing process, commonly used for packaging materials like foils, films, and wallpapers. Screen printing inks are utilized in screen printing, a method suitable for various substrates like textiles, ceramics, and glass. These different types cater to specific printing applications, providing diverse options for manufacturers and ensuring optimal results.</p></p>
