<p><p>The secondary aluminium alloy market consists of various types of alloys. Two common types are wrought alloys and case alloys. Wrought alloys undergo mechanical processes like rolling to improve their strength and shapeability. These alloys are often used in automobile parts, aircraft components, and construction materials. On the other hand, case alloys are specifically designed for applications requiring high strength and resistance to wear and corrosion, such as engine blocks and transmission cases.
