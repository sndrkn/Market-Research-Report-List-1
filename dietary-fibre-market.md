<p><strong>The Dietary Fibre Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Soluble Dietary Fiber</li><li>Water Insoluble Dietary Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dietary fiber market can be divided into two types: water-soluble dietary fiber and water-insoluble dietary fiber. Water-soluble dietary fiber dissolves in water and forms a gel-like substance in the digestive system, promoting healthy digestion and aiding in the prevention of certain diseases. Examples include pectin, beta-glucan, and psyllium. On the other hand, water-insoluble dietary fiber does not dissolve in water and passes through the digestive system mostly intact, aiding in regular bowel movements and preventing constipation. Examples include cellulose, hemicellulose, and lignin. These two types of dietary fiber offer different health benefits and play important roles in maintaining a balanced diet.</p></p>
