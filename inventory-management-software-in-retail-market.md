<p><p>Inventory management software in the retail market comes in two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based inventory management software operates on the internet and is accessible through any device with an internet connection. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as it requires no hardware installation or maintenance. On the other hand, on-premise inventory management software is installed directly on the retailer's computer servers. While it offers more control over data security, it typically involves higher upfront costs and requires regular maintenance and updates by the retailer's IT team.</p></p>
