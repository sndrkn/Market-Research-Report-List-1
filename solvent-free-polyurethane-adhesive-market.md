<p><p>Solvent-free polyurethane adhesive is categorized into two main types: monocomponent and bi-component. In the monocomponent market, the adhesive is ready to use and does not require any additional mixing or preparation before application. It offers convenient and efficient bonding solutions for various applications. On the other hand, the bi-component market requires the mixing of two different components, typically a resin and a hardener, before use. This allows for greater control over the curing process and enables the adhesive to bond a wider range of materials effectively.</p></p>
