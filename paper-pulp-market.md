<p><p>The paper pulp market is categorized into three main types: BSKP, BHKP, and BCTMP. BSKP stands for Bleached Softwood Kraft Pulp, which is made from softwood fibers and is widely used due to its high strength and brightness. BHKP refers to Bleached Hardwood Kraft Pulp, which is similar to BSKP but made from hardwood fibers. BCTMP, on the other hand, stands for Bleached Chemi-Thermomechanical Pulp and is produced using chemical and mechanical processes. These different types of pulp cater to various paper production needs and differ in terms of fiber source and manufacturing techniques.</p></p>
