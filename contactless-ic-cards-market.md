<p><p>Contactless IC cards, also known as smart cards, are a type of card technology that enables secure data transfer without physical contact. They are categorized into two types: memory cards and microprocessor cards. Memory cards store data on an embedded memory chip and are commonly used for simple tasks like storing predefined data or electronic cash. On the other hand, microprocessor cards feature a microprocessor chip, allowing for more complex computations, data processing, and multiple applications. These cards are commonly used for tasks like digital identification, access control, and financial transactions.</p></p>
