<p><p>Aliphatic isocyanates, such as HDI, IPDI, and H12MDI, are chemicals widely used in the production of various coatings and adhesives. HDI (hexamethylene diisocyanate) provides high durability and good weather resistance. IPDI (isophorone diisocyanate) offers fast curing and excellent chemical resistance. H12MDI (diphenylmethane diisocyanate) is known for its low viscosity and low color index, making it suitable for light-colored coatings. These types of isocyanates play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and properties of coatings and adhesives, meeting various industrial requirements.</p></p>
