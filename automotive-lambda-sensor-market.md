<p><p>The automotive lambda sensor market consists of two main types: titanium oxide type and zirconia type. Titanium oxide sensors are known for their ability to detect both oxygen concentration and temperature, allowing them to provide accurate data for engine management systems. On the other hand, zirconia sensors operate based on the principle of measuring the difference in oxygen concentration between the exhaust gas and the atmosphere. Both types play a crucial role in enabling vehicles to maintain optimal air-fuel mixture ratios, improving fuel efficiency, reducing emissions, and ensuring proper engine performance.</p></p>
