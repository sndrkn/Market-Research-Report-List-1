<p><p>Industrial alcohol, also known as denatured alcohol, is a type of alcohol that is primarily used for industrial purposes rather than for human consumption. One of the key drivers behind this growth is the increasing demand for renewable energy sources. This shift towards renewable energy is driving the demand for industrial alcohol.</p><p>Additionally, the use of industrial alcohol in the pharmaceutical industry is expected to contribute to market growth. Industrial alcohol is often used as a solvent in the manufacturing of various pharmaceutical drugs. With the growing pharmaceutical industry and the increasing demand for medicines globally, the demand for industrial alcohol as a solvent is also expected to rise.</p><p>Moreover, the expanding use of industrial alcohol in the cosmetics and personal care industry is expected to further fuel market growth.
