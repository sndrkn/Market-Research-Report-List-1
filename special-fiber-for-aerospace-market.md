<p><p>The aerospace market requires special fiber for various purposes. Conductive fiber is a type of fiber that has conductive properties, allowing it to be used in aerospace applications where electrical conductivity is required. This fiber helps in dissipating static electricity and reducing electromagnetic interference. On the other hand, insulation fiber provides thermal insulation for aerospace components. It helps in maintaining a stable temperature, protecting sensitive equipment from extreme heat or cold conditions. Both types of fibers play crucial roles in ensuring the safety and efficiency of aerospace systems.</p></p>
