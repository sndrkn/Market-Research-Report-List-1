<p><p>The car roof cargo carriers market includes two main types: roof boxes and roof baskets. Roof boxes are hardshell containers that offer protection for items being transported and are designed to fit securely on top of the car roof. They typically have a higher capacity and provide better weather resistance. On the other hand, roof baskets are open-style carriers that offer a more flexible storage solution. They are ideal for bulkier items or equipment that can withstand exposure to the elements. Both roof boxes and roof baskets cater to different needs and preferences of car owners.</p></p>
