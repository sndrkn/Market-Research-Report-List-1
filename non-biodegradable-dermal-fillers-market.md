<p><p>Non-biodegradable dermal fillers are utilized in cosmetic procedures to restore volume and smooth out wrinkles and lines on the face. There are various types available in the market. Calcium hydroxylapatite fillers are composed of microscopic particles suspended in a gel-like solution and stimulate collagen production. PMMA (polymethylmethacrylate) fillers consist of tiny beads that remain under the skin permanently and provide structural support. Other non-biodegradable fillers include silicone and polyalkylimide. These fillers offer long-lasting effects but do not naturally degrade over time, unlike their biodegradable counterparts.</p></p>
