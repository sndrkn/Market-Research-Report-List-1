<p><p>Forging is a metalworking process that involves shaping metal by applying compressive forces, typically through the use of hammers or presses. There are several types of forging markets, including closed die forgings, open die forgings, and rolled rings forgings. </p><p>Closed die forgings involve placing a piece of metal between two dies and applying pressure to shape it into the desired form. Open die forgings are created by heating the metal and using hammers or presses to shape it without enclosing dies. Rolled rings forgings are produced by shaping a circular metal piece into a ring through rolling or bending techniques.</p></p>
