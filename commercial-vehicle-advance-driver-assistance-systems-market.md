<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) include various types of technologies designed to enhance the safety and efficiency of commercial vehicles. Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) automatically adjusts the vehicle's speed to maintain a safe distance from the vehicle ahead. Blind Spot Detection (BSD) alerts the driver of any vehicles in their blind spots. Forward Collision Warning (FCW) warns the driver of potential collisions with vehicles in front. Lane Departure Warning System (LDWS) alerts the driver if they unintentionally veer out of their lane. Park Assist System (PAS) assists the driver in parking maneuvers. Other ADAS technologies may include features like driver fatigue monitoring and traffic sign recognition.</p></p>
