<p><p>The Plastic Bonded Magnets Market consists of two main types: Plastic Injected Magnets and Plastic Pressed Magnets. Plastic Injected Magnets are made by injecting a mixture of magnetic powder and plastic resin into molds. This process allows for complex shapes and intricate designs. Plastic Pressed Magnets are produced by compressing magnetic powder and plastic resin under high pressure. This method is more suitable for simpler shapes and higher volume production. Both types of magnets offer unique features and advantages, depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
