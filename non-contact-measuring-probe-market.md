<p><p>The non-contact measuring probe market can be divided into two main types: machine tool probes and coordinate measuring machine (CMM) probes. Machine tool probes are used for in-process measurement during machining operations, ensuring accuracy and reducing setup time. On the other hand, CMM probes are primarily used for dimensional inspection and metrology purposes, providing precise measurements of objects with complex geometries. Both types of probes contribute to improving efficiency, quality control, and reducing errors in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
