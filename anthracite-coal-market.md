<p><p>Anthracite coal market types can be categorized into two main segments: lump anthracite and anthracite fines. Lump anthracite refers to larger-sized coal pieces that are typically used in heating and industrial applications due to their high energy content and low volatile matter. On the other hand, anthracite fines are smaller-sized coal particles used mainly in the steelmaking industry. These fines possess superior carbon content and are utilized in the production of iron and steel, providing better combustion efficiency and reducing environmental emissions.</p></p>
