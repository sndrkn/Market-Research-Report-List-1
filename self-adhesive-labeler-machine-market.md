<p><p>Self-adhesive labeler machines are devices used to efficiently apply labels on products or packages. There are two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic labelers are fully automated, capable of labeling products without human intervention. They are ideal for high-volume production environments, ensuring fast and accurate labeling. On the other hand, semi-automatic labelers require some human interaction, such as manually placing the product or initiating the labeling process. Although not as fast as automatic labelers, these machines are more affordable and suitable for smaller production operations.</p></p>
