<p><p>Gasket sealant market comprises different types of sealants, such as liquid sealants and anaerobic sealants. Liquid sealants are formulated in liquid form and can be easily applied to the gasket surfaces. They harden when exposed to air, creating a reliable seal. On the other hand, anaerobic sealants are designed to cure in the absence of air. They are commonly used in applications where there is a need for high-performance seals, as they offer excellent chemical resistance and can withstand high temperatures and pressures.</p></p>
